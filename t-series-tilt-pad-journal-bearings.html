T Series Tilting Journal Pad (TJP) Bearings

Our range of tilting journal pad bearings can be designed and configured for any type of turbomachinery application. Journal pad bearings are suited for high speed, usually super-critical rotating machinery where stiffness and power loss are important factors in the design and rotordynamics.

Applications

Suitable for high speed turbomachinery such a compressors and turbines.


Size Range

T series bearings are available from 50mm up to 260mm shaft diameters. T Series bearings are available as 4 or 5 pad designs and can be designs as load between pads, or load on pad.


Materials

Tilt pads can be manufactured from steel or copper-chrome-zirconium (CCZ) backing with BS3332-A (ASTM B23 Grade 2) whitemetal. Pivot design can be cyclindrical or spherical pivots to help with misalignment.

Instrumentation

Pre-machined pads ready to accept your instrumentation or complete supply of bearings with instrumentation installed.
