A Trusted Partner for Critical Rotating Equipment — From OEM Supply to Failure Recovery

Oiltech supports OEMs, service providers and operators with precision manufacturing, rapid repair and engineering solutions for hydrodynamic bearings used in the world's most demanding industrial applications.

Oiltech is a specialist engineering company focused on hydrodynamic bearings and rotating equipment.

We combine precision manufacturing, advanced engineering capability and rapid repair and recovery services to support customers across the full lifecycle of critical components.

What We Do

We provide integrated solutions across:

Manufacturing

OEM supply and make-to-print production of high-performance bearings.

Repair & Refurbishment

Rapid restoration of damaged components to minimise downtime.

Engineering & Analysis

Design, optimisation and failure diagnosis for improved performance and reliability.

Re-Engineering

Replacement and upgrade of obsolete or underperforming components.

Our Position

We operate at the intersection of OEM manufacturing capability, aftermarket repair and recovery, and engineering analysis and optimisation.

Enabling customers to manage both planned supply and unexpected failures through a single trusted partner.
