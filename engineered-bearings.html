Engineered Solutions

Oiltech Bearings provides engineered solutions for new equipment and as an alternative spare part sourcing.



Re-Engineering

When sourcing obsolete, long lead and sometimes high cost spares, re-engineering provides a solution. A robust process to inspect part and determine the original design generates a new production drawing for the part, which can be manufactured by Oiltech Bearings.

Design and Analysis

New applications and upgrades require a mechanical design and performance prediction of the bearing oil film to determine that the bearing operation will be safe and reliable.

Standard Product Ranges

Although these are Interchangeable and can supplied as standard, these product ranges also allow quick selection of a suitable design and if need be customised to requirements.
