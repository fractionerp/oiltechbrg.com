J Series Equalising Thrust Bearings.

Equalising or self-levelling thrust bearings have significant advantages over standard tilt pad design. The series of internal segments that link the pads together, allow for perfect alignment or levelling of the thrust face. This ensure the thrust pads can share the load equally and operate at higher overall loads.
Our J Series equalising bearings are inter-changeable with common designs in the market place and can be made from standard steel backed or copper-chrome backed thrust pads.

Applications

Suitable for pumps, gearboxes, motors and small steam turbines.


Size Range

J Series bearings are available from 5" up to 23" diameter.


Materials

Tilt pads can be manufactured from bronze, steel or copper-chrome-zirconium (CCZ) backing with BS3332-A (ASTM B23 Grade 2) whitemetal.

Instrumentation

Pre-machined pads ready to accept your instrumentation or complete supply of bearings with instrumentation installed.
