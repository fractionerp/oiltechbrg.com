How To Select The Right Bearing

Choosing the correct bearing depends on your application requirements.

Key Selection Factors

  • Load and speed
  • Equipment type (turbine, compressor, pump)
  • Lubrication method
  • Stability and vibration requirements

Different bearing types are suited to different applications — selecting the right configuration is critical to performance and reliability.

Product Range

A Series

Fixed profile journal bearing. Cylindrical, elliptical, offset, 3 lobed or 4 lobed bore profile.

B Series

Fixed profile journal bearing with flanges. Cylindrical, elliptical, offset, 3 lobed or 4 lobed bore profile.

C Series

Fixed profile journal bearing with single or double location faces. Plain or tapered location faces.

J Series

Equalising 6 pad thrust bearings. Hardened spherical pivots with steel, bronze or CCZ backing.

M Series

Modular thrust bearings. Flooded or direct lubrication.

T Series

Tilt pad journal bearings. Flooded or direct lubrication. Centre or offset pivot journal pads.

Product Details

Fixed Profile Journal Bearings (A, B, C Series)

Designed for a wide range of industrial applications requiring robust and reliable radial support.

  • Pumps, gearboxes and lower speed rotating equipment
  • Cylindrical, elliptical (lemon bore) and offset designs
  • Multi-lobe (3 or 4 lobe) profiles
  • Flanged and location face options

Tilt Pad Journal Bearings (T Series)

Used in high-speed and high-performance turbomachinery.

  • Steam and gas turbines, compressors
  • Tilting pads for improved stability
  • Flooded or directed lubrication
  • Centre or offset pivot configurations

Thrust Bearings (J & M Series)

Designed to manage axial loads in rotating equipment.

  • J Series — Equalising thrust bearings for load sharing across multiple pads
  • M Series — Modular thrust bearings for flexible configurations
  • Critical for controlling axial movement and load distribution

Combined Bearing Assemblies

Integrated solutions combining radial and axial load support.

  • Journal + thrust bearing combinations
  • Compact, application-specific designs
  • Used in turbines and compressors

Seals & Auxiliary Components

Supporting system performance and reliability:

  • Labyrinth seals
  • Oil rings and deflectors
  • Lead-lined and specialised sealing solutions

Materials & Performance

All bearings are manufactured using high-quality materials suitable for demanding applications.

Whitemetal Linings

Whitemetal (Babbitt) linings to BS3332 / ASTM B23 standards.

Backing Materials

Steel and bronze backings. Copper-chrome-zirconium (CCZ) for high load and high speed applications.

Instrumentation

All products can be machined and supplied with thermocouples or RTDs.
