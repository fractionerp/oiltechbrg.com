Inspection & Verification

Each component undergoes structured inspection and verification to ensure full compliance with specification.

Dimensional Inspection

CMM and manual inspection to confirm all critical dimensions.

Non-Destructive Testing

Ultrasonic testing (UT) to ISO 4386-1. Dye penetrant testing (PT) to ISO 4386-3.

Material Certification

Full material traceability with certificates to EN 10204 3.1, linking each component to its original material heat and batch.

Final Visual Inspection

Comprehensive visual inspection prior to dispatch.

Inspection is integrated throughout the manufacturing process — not treated as a final step.
