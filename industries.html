Engineering, Manufacturing and Repair Solutions for Critical Rotating Equipment Across Multiple Industries

Oiltech supports operators, OEMs and service providers in industries where reliability, performance and downtime are critical. From turbines and compressors to heavy industrial machinery, we deliver solutions tailored to real operating environments.

Speak to an Engineer Request Support

Our expertise is focused on hydrodynamic bearings used in critical rotating equipment.

Rather than serving industries in isolation, we support the machines and applications that drive them — ensuring reliability, performance and rapid response when issues occur.

Industries We Support

Oil & Gas

Applications: Compressors, gas turbines, pumps

Challenges: High-speed operation and dynamic instability, continuous operation with minimal downtime tolerance, harsh operating environments.

  • Bearing repair during critical outages
  • Design optimisation for stability and performance
  • Re-engineering of obsolete or long-lead-time components

Power Generation

Applications: Steam turbines, generators, auxiliary systems

Challenges: High load and continuous operation, temperature management and thermal stability, ageing equipment and legacy designs.

  • Repair and refurbishment of turbine bearings
  • Performance optimisation and re-engineering
  • Support for legacy and obsolete equipment

Mining & Heavy Industry

Applications: Mills and crushers, gearboxes, heavy rotating equipment

Challenges: High loads and harsh environments, contamination and wear, maintenance-driven downtime.

  • Manufacture and repair of large bearing components
  • Material selection for durability
  • Rapid response to minimise downtime

Petrochemical & Process Industries

Applications: Pumps, compressors, process machinery

Challenges: Continuous operation requirements, sensitivity to failure and unplanned shutdowns, corrosive or chemically aggressive environments.

  • Bearing repair and replacement solutions
  • Material and design optimisation
  • Troubleshooting and failure analysis

Marine & Offshore

Applications: Propulsion systems, auxiliary rotating equipment

Challenges: Corrosive environments, load variability, reliability in remote locations.

  • Durable bearing solutions for harsh environments
  • Repair and refurbishment services
  • Re-engineering for extended service life

General Industry

Applications: Pumps, fans and blowers, industrial machinery

Challenges: Cost vs performance balance, maintenance and reliability requirements, variable operating conditions.

  • Standard and custom bearing solutions
  • Repair and refurbishment
  • Engineering support for performance improvement

Bearing failure and downtime | OEM lead times and obsolescence | Performance limitations

Lubrication and thermal issues | Vibration and instability
