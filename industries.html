Industries We Serve

Oiltech Bearings provides whitemetal bearing manufacturing, repair, and re-engineering services to a wide range of industries worldwide. Our expertise spans over 70 years of experience in hydrodynamic bearing technology.

Power Generation

We supply bearings for steam turbines, gas turbines, generators, and auxiliary equipment in fossil fuel, nuclear, and renewable power plants. Our rapid response service minimizes downtime during planned outages and emergency shutdowns.

  • Steam turbine journal and thrust bearings
  • Generator bearings
  • Exciter bearings
  • Boiler feed pump bearings

Oil & Gas

Supporting upstream, midstream, and downstream operations with bearings for compressors, pumps, and turbomachinery used in refineries, petrochemical plants, and offshore platforms.

  • Centrifugal compressor bearings
  • Pipeline pump bearings
  • Gas turbine bearings
  • Reciprocating compressor bearings

Marine & Shipbuilding

Providing bearings for propulsion systems, auxiliary machinery, and deck equipment on commercial vessels, naval ships, and offshore installations.

  • Main propulsion turbine bearings
  • Stern tube bearings
  • Gearbox bearings
  • Auxiliary pump and fan bearings

Pumps & Compressors

Manufacturing and repairing bearings for all types of rotating pumps and compressors used in water treatment, chemical processing, and industrial applications.

  • Vertical pump bearings
  • Horizontal split case pump bearings
  • Screw compressor bearings
  • Centrifugal pump bearings

Steel & Metals

Supplying bearings for rolling mills, furnace fans, and heavy industrial equipment in steel plants and metal processing facilities.

  • Rolling mill bearings
  • Furnace fan bearings
  • Crane and hoist bearings
  • Heavy-duty gearbox bearings

Mining & Cement

Providing durable bearing solutions for crushers, mills, kilns, and material handling equipment operating in demanding environments.

  • Ball mill and SAG mill bearings
  • Kiln support bearings
  • Crusher bearings
  • Conveyor drive bearings

Paper & Pulp

Supporting paper mills with bearings for refiners, pumps, and drying equipment that must withstand continuous operation and harsh conditions.

  • Refiner bearings
  • Press roll bearings
  • Vacuum pump bearings
  • Fan and blower bearings
