What We Manufacture

Journal Bearings (Fixed Profile)

Precision journal bearings for turbines, compressors and heavy rotating equipment.

  • Cylindrical, elliptical (lemon bore), offset and multi-lobe designs
  • Hydrostatic jacking / lifting capability
  • Designed for high-load industrial applications

Tilt Pad Journal Bearings

High-performance bearings for high-speed turbomachinery.

  • 4 and 5 pad configurations
  • Flooded or directed lubrication
  • Preload optimisation
  • Advanced materials including copper-chrome-zirconium (CCZ)

Thrust Bearings

Designed to manage axial loads in demanding applications.

  • Tilt pad thrust bearings (modular design)
  • Equalising thrust bearings for load distribution
  • Centre and offset pivot designs
  • High load capacity configurations

Combined Bearings

Integrated journal and thrust bearing assemblies for compact and high-performance systems.

Seals & Auxiliary Components

Supporting components to ensure optimal system performance:

  • Floating seals and oil rings
  • Labyrinth seals
  • Lead-lined and balance piston seals

Large & Specialised Components

Manufacturing and refurbishment of large-scale components for heavy industry:

  • Mill bearing pads
  • Compressor crossheads
  • Gearbox pinions
