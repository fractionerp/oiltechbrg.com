Bearing Solutions for Petrochemical & Process Industries

Engineering-led hydrodynamic bearings for compressors, pumps and process machinery in refineries, chemical plants and continuous production environments.

Speak to an Engineer Request a Quote

Petrochemical and process industries run critical rotating equipment around the clock. Unplanned shutdowns can cost millions and create safety risks. Bearing reliability is not optional — it's fundamental to safe, continuous operation.

Applications & Challenges

We support operators, maintenance teams and turnaround contractors in refineries, chemical plants and process facilities.

Typical Applications

  • Centrifugal compressors (ethylene crackers, ammonia, refrigeration)
  • Process pumps (API 610 and critical service)
  • Steam turbine drives
  • Fans and blowers
  • Gearboxes in process trains

Key Challenges

  • Continuous operation with minimal downtime tolerance
  • Corrosive and chemically aggressive environments
  • Tight turnaround and shutdown schedules
  • Sensitivity to vibration and instability
  • Ageing plant with legacy bearing designs
