Power generation relies on continuous, stable operation of turbines and generators. Bearing performance directly impacts plant availability, efficiency and safety. Whether supporting aging fleets or new installations, Oiltech delivers bearing solutions designed for the demands of modern power generation.

Power Generation Bearing Challenges

Power plants demand bearing solutions that deliver continuous reliability, thermal stability and long service life under high loads.

Typical Applications

  • Steam turbines (coal, gas, biomass, waste-to-energy, nuclear)
  • Gas turbines (simple cycle, combined cycle, peaker plants)
  • Generators and exciters
  • Boiler feed pumps
  • Cooling water pumps

Key Challenges

  • Continuous high-load operation
  • Temperature management and thermal stability
  • Aging equipment requiring lifecycle extension
  • Energy transition increasing cycling demands
  • Long lead times during planned outages
