Bearing Solutions for Marine & Offshore

Engineering-led hydrodynamic bearings for propulsion systems, turbines and auxiliary equipment on vessels, platforms and offshore installations.

Speak to an Engineer Request Urgent Support

Marine and offshore environments are among the most demanding for rotating equipment. Corrosion, load variability, vibration, and the critical need for reliability in remote locations all place exceptional demands on bearing performance. Oiltech provides engineering-led solutions designed to withstand these conditions.

Marine & Offshore Bearing Challenges

Vessels, platforms and offshore installations demand bearing solutions that perform reliably in harsh, remote environments with limited access to specialist support.

Typical Applications

  • Propulsion shaft bearings and stern tube bearings
  • Marine turbines (steam, gas)
  • Marine gearbox bearings
  • Diesel engine bearings (large two-stroke)
  • Auxiliary rotating equipment (pumps, compressors, generators)
  • LNG carrier turbomachinery

Key Challenges

  • Corrosive saltwater environments
  • Load variability from sea state and manoeuvring
  • Reliability critical in remote/at-sea locations
  • Long vessel lifecycles requiring bearing longevity
  • Limited access to specialist support during voyages
  • Classification society requirements
