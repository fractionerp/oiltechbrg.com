Engineering Analysis, Failure Diagnosis and Performance Optimisation for Critical Rotating Equipment

Oiltech provides engineering-led technical services to solve complex bearing and rotating equipment challenges.

Combining practical experience with advanced analysis tools, we support clients in diagnosing failures, improving performance and ensuring long-term reliability of critical machinery.

When To Engage Oiltech

Our technical services are typically required when:

  • A bearing failure has occurred and the root cause is unclear
  • Equipment is experiencing vibration, instability or overheating
  • Performance is below expected levels
  • A design is no longer suitable for current operating conditions
  • A new or modified application requires engineering validation

Core Technical Capabilities

Failure Analysis & Root Cause Investigation

Understanding why a bearing failed is critical to preventing recurrence.

  • Detailed inspection and failure mode identification
  • Analysis of operating conditions and system behaviour
  • Identification of underlying causes (not just symptoms)
  • Practical recommendations to prevent repeat failures

Bearing Design & Performance Analysis

Engineering support for both new and existing applications.

  • Bearing geometry design and optimisation
  • Load, speed and lubrication evaluation
  • Application-specific performance modelling
  • Verification of operating limits and safety margins

Oil Film & Performance Modelling

Advanced analysis of hydrodynamic bearing behaviour.

  • Oil film pressure and temperature distribution
  • Minimum film thickness evaluation
  • Power loss and thermal performance
  • Stability characteristics and damping behaviour

This enables optimisation of bearing performance in high-speed turbomachinery.

Re-Engineering & Design Optimisation

Improving performance beyond original specifications.

  • Redesign of existing bearings
  • Upgrades for increased load or speed
  • Modification of lubrication systems
  • Adaptation to changing operating conditions
