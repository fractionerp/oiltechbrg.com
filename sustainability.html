Sustainability

Oiltech Bearings is committed to minimising its impact on the environment and reducing its consumption of resources.

Energy Consumption

The company has subscribed to offset 20 tons per year of CO2 through Carbon Neutral Britain

The next step the company will take will be to purchase its energy from 100% carbon neutral energy sources.

Recycling

Where it is possible our products are packaged with materials that are easily recycled and we re-use packaging as much as possible.
