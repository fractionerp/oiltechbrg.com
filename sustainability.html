Environmental Sustainability

Reducing impact through responsible operations.

Oiltech is committed to minimising the environmental impact of its operations.

Our approach focuses on responsible manufacturing, efficient use of materials and continuous improvement in how we manage energy, waste and resources.

Our Approach

We take a practical approach to environmental sustainability, focusing on areas where we can make a measurable difference.

  • Reducing material waste
  • Managing energy use efficiently
  • Operating safe and controlled processes
  • Continuously improving environmental performance

Key Focus Areas

Material Use & Waste Reduction

  • Efficient use of raw materials
  • Reducing scrap through controlled processes
  • Recycling materials where possible

Energy & Operations

  • Optimising production processes
  • Reducing unnecessary energy consumption
  • Maintaining equipment for efficient operation

Environmental Controls

  • Proper handling and storage of materials
  • Waste management in line with regulations
  • Maintaining clean and safe working environments

Compliance & Standards

Oiltech operates in line with relevant environmental regulations and industry standards.

We are committed to maintaining compliance and continually reviewing our processes to improve performance.

Continuous Improvement

We regularly assess our operations to identify opportunities to:

Reduce Impact

Minimise environmental impact across all activities.

Improve Efficiency

Optimise processes for better resource utilisation.

Strengthen Practices

Build on existing environmental practices continuously.

We recognise that environmental responsibility is an ongoing process. Our focus is on making consistent, practical improvements across all areas of the business.
