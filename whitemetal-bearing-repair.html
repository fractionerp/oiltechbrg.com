When Failure Happens

Whitemetal bearings are designed to fail first — protecting the rotor and critical components of your machine.

But when they do fail, the cost of downtime escalates rapidly.

Unplanned outage? Bearing wipe or damage? OEM replacement lead time too long?

Oiltech provides rapid, engineered repair solutions to return your equipment to service quickly and safely.

Repair Process

Every repair follows a robust, quality-controlled process.

Quality-Controlled Restoration

  • Inspection and condition assessment
  • Removal of damaged whitemetal lining
  • Surface preparation and re-lining
  • Precision machining and re-sizing
  • Ultrasonic and dye penetrant inspection
  • Final machining and dimensional verification
  • Preservation and delivery

All repairs follow the same rigorous inspection standards as new manufacturing.

  • Electrical discharge damage
